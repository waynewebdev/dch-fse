Our Story

How a Family Legacy Became Central Texas’ Trusted Home Builder

Joshua Maas didn’t choose home building — it chose him. Growing up alongside his grandfather on job sites across San Antonio, Josh learned that building a home is about more than lumber and concrete. It’s about trust, craftsmanship, and delivering on your word. Those lessons became the foundation of Dynamic Custom Homes when Josh launched the company in 2007.

Nearly two decades later, Dynamic Custom Homes has built over 100 custom homes across Central Texas — from San Antonio and Boerne to New Braunfels and the Texas Hill Country. Josh still personally oversees every project, and our clients become extended family. We build homes that families grow into, create memories in, and pass down for generations.

Meet the Builder

Joshua Maas — Owner & Custom Home Builder

Josh Maas is the owner and hands-on builder behind Dynamic Custom Homes. He grew up in the San Antonio home building industry, spending weekends and summers learning the trade alongside his grandfather — a respected custom home builder in the area. That early education in craftsmanship, work ethic, and doing things right became the foundation of his career.

With nearly 20 years of experience and over 100 custom homes built, Josh and his team have earned the trust of families across San Antonio, Boerne, New Braunfels, and the Texas Hill Country. Whether you’re building your first custom home or your forever home, Josh treats every project like it’s his own — because to him, your home is personal.

How We Build

Our Custom Home Building Process

  1. 01

    Design Consultation & Planning

    Every project begins with a one-on-one conversation about your vision, lifestyle, and budget. We listen first, then work with you to develop a custom floor plan, select materials and finishes, and create a detailed project timeline.

  2. 02

    Custom Home Construction

    With Josh personally managing your build, you’ll have direct communication and full visibility throughout construction. We coordinate every trade, manage the schedule, and provide regular updates so you’re never in the dark.

  3. 03

    Final Walkthrough & Move-In

    Before we hand over the keys, we walk every inch of your new home together. We address any final details and make sure everything meets our standards — and yours. We don’t consider a project complete until you’re completely satisfied.
